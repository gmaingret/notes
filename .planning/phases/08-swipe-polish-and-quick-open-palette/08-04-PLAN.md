    Phase 8 — Swipe Polish and Quick-Open Palette, fully deployed to https://notes.gregorymaingret.fr

    **Swipe animations (GEST-01..05):**
    - Swiping a bullet reveals a green (right) or red (left) backing with an icon that scales up proportionally as you drag toward the 40% threshold
    - When drag reaches the 40% threshold, the icon briefly pulses larger to signal the action is locked in
    - Releasing before the threshold snaps the row back with a smooth ease-out animation (no spring/overshoot)
    - Releasing at or past the threshold slides the full row off screen in the swipe direction before it disappears from the list
    - Swipe-right on an already-completed bullet toggles it back to incomplete

    **Quick-Open Palette (QKOP-01..07):**
    - Ctrl+K (or Cmd+K on Mac) opens the palette from anywhere in the app — document list, open document, bookmarks view
    - Empty state shows 5 most recently opened documents
    - Typing ≥2 characters shows grouped results: Documents (title match), Bullets (content search), Bookmarks
    - Arrow Up / Arrow Down navigate results across sections; Enter opens the selected result
    - Escape or clicking outside the palette closes it
    - On mobile: a search icon button is visible in the document header next to the hamburger — tapping it opens the palette
